# Industrial Engineers — Career Brief

**Type:** O*NET / BLS career reference
**SOC code:** 17-2112.00
**Typical salary band:** $70,000 – $120,000
**Outlook:** 25.0% projected growth

## Top skills
- Reading Comprehension
- Monitoring
- Active Listening

## A day in the life
Estimate production costs, cost saving methods, and the effects of product design changes on expenditures for management review, action, and control. Plan and establish sequence of operations to fabricate and assemble parts or products and to promote efficient utilization.

## Best-fit interests (RIASEC)
- Investigative (research, analysis)
- Conventional (organizing, detail)
- Enterprising (leading, persuading)

## How to use this brief
Use this as grounded context when assessing readiness for **Industrial Engineers**: compare a candidate's skills against the top skills above, anchor salary and growth expectations to the band shown, and tailor interview focus to the day-in-the-life responsibilities.
