# Sales Engineers — Career Brief

**Type:** O*NET / BLS career reference
**SOC code:** 41-9031.00
**Typical salary band:** $70,000 – $120,000
**Outlook:** 25.0% projected growth

## Top skills
- Reading Comprehension
- Speaking
- Persuasion

## A day in the life
Collaborate with sales teams to understand customer requirements, to promote the sale of company products, and to provide sales support. Create sales or service contracts for products or services.

## Best-fit interests (RIASEC)
- Enterprising (leading, persuading)
- Social (helping, teaching)
- Investigative (research, analysis)

## How to use this brief
Use this as grounded context when assessing readiness for **Sales Engineers**: compare a candidate's skills against the top skills above, anchor salary and growth expectations to the band shown, and tailor interview focus to the day-in-the-life responsibilities.
