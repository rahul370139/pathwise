# Computer and Information Research Scientists — Career Brief

**Type:** O*NET / BLS career reference
**SOC code:** 15-1221.00
**Typical salary band:** $60,000 – $100,000
**Outlook:** 15.0% projected growth

## Top skills
- Active Learning
- Systems Evaluation
- Judgment and Decision Making

## A day in the life
Analyze problems to develop solutions involving computer hardware and software. Apply theoretical expertise and innovation to create or apply new technology, such as adapting principles for applying computers to new uses.

## Best-fit interests (RIASEC)
- Investigative (research, analysis)
- Artistic (creative, design)
- Conventional (organizing, detail)
