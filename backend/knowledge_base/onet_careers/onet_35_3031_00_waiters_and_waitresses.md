# Waiters and Waitresses — Career Brief

**Type:** O*NET / BLS career reference
**SOC code:** 35-3031.00
**Typical salary band:** $60,000 – $100,000
**Outlook:** 35.0% projected growth

## Top skills
- Active Listening
- Speaking
- Service Orientation

## A day in the life
Check with customers to ensure that they are enjoying their meals, and take action to correct any problems. Write patrons' food orders on order slips, memorize orders, or enter orders into computers for transmittal to kitchen staff.

## Best-fit interests (RIASEC)
- Social (helping, teaching)
- Enterprising (leading, persuading)
- Artistic (creative, design)

## How to use this brief
Use this as grounded context when assessing readiness for **Waiters and Waitresses**: compare a candidate's skills against the top skills above, anchor salary and growth expectations to the band shown, and tailor interview focus to the day-in-the-life responsibilities.
