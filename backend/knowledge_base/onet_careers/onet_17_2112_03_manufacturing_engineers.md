# Manufacturing Engineers — Career Brief

**Type:** O*NET / BLS career reference
**SOC code:** 17-2112.03
**Typical salary band:** $70,000 – $120,000
**Outlook:** 25.0% projected growth

## Top skills
- Reading Comprehension
- Monitoring
- Judgment and Decision Making

## A day in the life
Troubleshoot new or existing product problems involving designs, materials, or processes. Identify opportunities or implement changes to improve manufacturing processes or products or to reduce costs, using knowledge of fabrication processes, tooling and production equipment, assembly methods, quality control standards, or product design, materials and parts.

## Best-fit interests (RIASEC)
- Investigative (research, analysis)
- Realistic (hands-on, building)
- Artistic (creative, design)

## How to use this brief
Use this as grounded context when assessing readiness for **Manufacturing Engineers**: compare a candidate's skills against the top skills above, anchor salary and growth expectations to the band shown, and tailor interview focus to the day-in-the-life responsibilities.
