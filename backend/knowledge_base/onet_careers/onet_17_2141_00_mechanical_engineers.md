# Mechanical Engineers — Career Brief

**Type:** O*NET / BLS career reference
**SOC code:** 17-2141.00
**Typical salary band:** $70,000 – $120,000
**Outlook:** 25.0% projected growth

## Top skills
- Reading Comprehension
- Mathematics
- Science

## A day in the life
Read and interpret blueprints, technical drawings, schematics, or computer-generated reports. Research, design, evaluate, install, operate, or maintain mechanical products, equipment, systems or processes to meet requirements.

## Best-fit interests (RIASEC)
- Investigative (research, analysis)
- Realistic (hands-on, building)
- Artistic (creative, design)
