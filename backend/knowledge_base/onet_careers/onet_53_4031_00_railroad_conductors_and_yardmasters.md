# Railroad Conductors and Yardmasters — Career Brief

**Type:** O*NET / BLS career reference

## A day in the life
Signal engineers to begin train runs, stop trains, or change speed, using telecommunications equipment or hand signals. Confer with engineers regarding train routes, timetables, and cargoes, and to discuss alternative routes when there are rail defects or obstructions.

## Best-fit interests (RIASEC)

- Enterprising (leading, persuading)
- Conventional (organizing, detail)
