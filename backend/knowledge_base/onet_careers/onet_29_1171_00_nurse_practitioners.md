# Nurse Practitioners — Career Brief

**Type:** O*NET / BLS career reference
**SOC code:** 29-1171.00
**Typical salary band:** $60,000 – $100,000
**Outlook:** 15.0% projected growth

## Top skills
- Reading Comprehension
- Active Listening
- Social Perceptiveness

## A day in the life
Maintain complete and detailed records of patients' health care plans and prognoses. Develop treatment plans, based on scientific rationale, standards of care, and professional practice guidelines.

## Best-fit interests (RIASEC)
- Social (helping, teaching)
- Investigative (research, analysis)
- Realistic (hands-on, building)

## How to use this brief
