# Computer and Information Systems Managers — Career Brief

**Type:** O*NET / BLS career reference
**SOC code:** 11-3021.00
**Typical salary band:** $90,000 – $150,000
**Outlook:** 15.0% projected growth

## Top skills
- Monitoring
- Critical Thinking
- Reading Comprehension

## A day in the life
Direct daily operations of department, analyzing workflow, establishing priorities, developing standards and setting deadlines. Meet with department heads, managers, supervisors, vendors, and others, to solicit cooperation and resolve problems.

## Best-fit interests (RIASEC)
- Enterprising (leading, persuading)
- Conventional (organizing, detail)
- Investigative (research, analysis)

## How to use this brief
Use this as grounded context when assessing readiness for **Computer and Information Systems Managers**: compare a candidate's skills against the top skills above, anchor salary and growth expectations to the band shown, and tailor interview focus to the day-in-the-life responsibilities.
