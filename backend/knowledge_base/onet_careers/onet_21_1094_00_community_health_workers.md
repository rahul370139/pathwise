# Community Health Workers — Career Brief

**Type:** O*NET / BLS career reference
**SOC code:** 21-1094.00
**Typical salary band:** $60,000 – $100,000
**Outlook:** 15.0% projected growth

## Top skills

## A day in the life
Advise clients or community groups on issues related to improving general health, such as diet or exercise. Advise clients or community groups on issues related to diagnostic screenings, such as breast cancer screening, pap smears, glaucoma tests, or diabetes screenings.

- Artistic (creative, design)
- Enterprising (leading, persuading)
