# Agricultural Engineers — Career Brief

**Type:** O*NET / BLS career reference
**SOC code:** 17-2021.00
**Typical salary band:** $70,000 – $120,000

## A day in the life
Prepare reports, sketches, working drawings, specifications, proposals, and budgets for proposed sites or systems. Visit sites to observe environmental problems, to consult with contractors, or to monitor construction activities.
