# Computer Systems Analysts — Career Brief

**Type:** O*NET / BLS career reference
**SOC code:** 15-1211.00
**Typical salary band:** $70,000 – $120,000
**Outlook:** 15.0% projected growth

## Top skills
- Reading Comprehension
- Judgment and Decision Making
- Systems Evaluation

## A day in the life
Troubleshoot program and system malfunctions to restore normal functioning. Provide staff and users with assistance solving computer-related problems, such as malfunctions and program problems.

## Best-fit interests (RIASEC)
- Investigative (research, analysis)
- Conventional (organizing, detail)
- Artistic (creative, design)

## How to use this brief
Use this as grounded context when assessing readiness for **Computer Systems Analysts**: compare a candidate's skills against the top skills above, anchor salary and growth expectations to the band shown, and tailor interview focus to the day-in-the-life responsibilities.
