**Type:** O*NET / BLS career reference
**SOC code:** 19-4051.02

## A day in the life
Analyze samples, such as air or water samples, for contaminants or other elements. Prepare reports describing contamination tests, material or equipment decontaminated, or methods used in decontamination processes.
