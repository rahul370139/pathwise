# Critical Care Nurses — Career Brief

**Type:** O*NET / BLS career reference
**SOC code:** 29-1141.03
**Typical salary band:** $60,000 – $100,000
**Outlook:** 15.0% projected growth

## Top skills
- Critical Thinking
- Monitoring
- Social Perceptiveness

## A day in the life
Evaluate patients' vital signs or laboratory data to determine emergency intervention needs. Administer medications intravenously, by injection, orally, through gastric tubes, or by other methods.

- Investigative (research, analysis)
- Realistic (hands-on, building)
