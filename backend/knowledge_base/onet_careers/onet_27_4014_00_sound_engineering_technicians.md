# Sound Engineering Technicians — Career Brief

**Type:** O*NET / BLS career reference
**SOC code:** 27-4014.00
**Typical salary band:** $70,000 – $120,000
**Outlook:** 25.0% projected growth

## Top skills
- Active Listening
- Speaking
- Critical Thinking

## A day in the life
Record speech, music, and other sounds on recording media, using recording equipment. Confer with producers, performers, and others to determine and achieve the desired sound for a production, such as a musical recording or a film.

## Best-fit interests (RIASEC)
- Artistic (creative, design)
- Realistic (hands-on, building)
- Social (helping, teaching)
